About Zontrmontro

Empowering young minds with future-ready skills

Creative Learning

Zontrmontro is a creative weekend learning initiative in Bangladesh that empowers school going kids with future-ready skills like robotics, programming, web development, and artificial intelligence.

Building Confidence

Our sessions are designed to spark curiosity, build confidence, and help children become creators of technology. We believe in nurturing young minds for a better tomorrow.

Mission Driven

Our mission is to build a self-reliant, innovation-driven Bangladesh by nurturing young minds today. We want kids to create robots and innovative products for our country's development.

Friday Schooling

Revolutionary learning that turns Fridays into innovation days

Hands-On Learning

No exams, no lectures! Students engage in hands-on classes covering robotics, programming, AI, game development, and web development.

Foster Creativity

Friday Schooling fosters creativity in a tech-driven space where kids can explore, experiment, and build amazing projects.
